Proficient Tools Experience

Utilized various digital forensics tools to conduct comprehensive examinations of digital evidence and devices as a Digital Forensics Analyst for law enforcement (Income Tax) in Mumbai. Autopsy was employed for comprehensive disk analysis, file recovery, and timeline creation to uncover critical evidence. Cellebrite UFED enabled the extraction and analysis of mobile device data, facilitating the recovery of deleted messages, call logs, and application data. Oxygen Forensics was leveraged to analyze smartphone data, apps, and cloud services, providing in-depth insights into user activity. EnCase Forensics was applied for detailed investigation and reporting, including file system analysis and evidence preservation. These experiences built a strong foundation in digital forensics methodologies, enhancing capabilities in evidence recovery, analysis, and reporting for complex investigations.
